**Caption (EN):**
There's a hidden charge on your electricity bill that most people ignore. It's called Ft.

Ft stands for Fuel Adjustment Charge. PEA adjusts it every 4 months based on the cost of natural gas, coal, and diesel used to generate Thailand's electricity.

You can't control it. You can't predict it. And it's been trending up for years.

In 2020, Ft was negative (a discount). In 2024, it hit ฿0.39 per kWh. In 2026, it sits around ฿0.33.

On a ฿30,000 monthly bill, Ft alone adds ฿1,500-2,000.

Solar eliminates your exposure to Ft. Every kWh you generate from your roof costs ฿0 — no fuel adjustment, no surprises, no government policy changes.

That's not just savings. That's energy price certainty for 25+ years.
