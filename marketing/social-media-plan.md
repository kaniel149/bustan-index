## Research Findings

### Thai Solar Social Media Landscape
- **Top-performing content types:** Before/after installation photos, savings breakdowns with real numbers, time-lapse installation videos, educational carousels explaining net billing
- **Competitor analysis (High Solar — Pattaya):** European-managed solar company targeting expats and businesses. Uses project showcases, testimonials, and CO2 impact stats. Professional but somewhat corporate. Opportunity: Bustan Energy can differentiate with island lifestyle integration and bilingual content
- **Content gaps in Thai solar market:** Very few companies do bilingual EN/TH content well. Most Thai solar pages are either all-Thai (targeting locals) or all-English (targeting expats). Serving both simultaneously is a strong differentiator
- **What performs well:** Real project photos with specific kWp numbers, savings calculations with actual THB figures, team photos (humanizes the brand), educational content about PEA net billing (high save/share rate)

### Koh Phangan Local Context
- **Electricity pain point:** Island runs on mainland grid + diesel backup. Power outages common during storms. Bills are high — hotels/resorts pay ฿50,000-200,000/month
- **Expat community:** Large international community (European, American, Australian). Active on Facebook groups (Koh Phangan Community, Koh Phangan Expats). Eco-conscious, sustainability-minded
- **Tourism angle:** Eco-tourism is growing. "100% Solar Powered" is a selling point on Booking.com and Airbnb. Travelers actively choose sustainable stays
- **Property boom:** 8.9% annual property value growth. Solar adds 5-10% to property value — relevant for investors and developers

### Thai Electricity Rates & Solar Economics
- **PEA retail rate:** ~฿6/kWh (varies by usage tier, higher tiers pay more)
- **Net billing export rate:** ฿2.70/kWh (sell excess back to grid)
- **Self-consumption value:** ฿6/kWh (every kWh you use from solar saves full retail rate)
- **Peak sun hours (Koh Phangan):** 4.2 hours/day average
- **EPC pricing:** ~฿29/Wp
- **PPA rate (Bustan Energy):** ฿4.50/kWh (25% below PEA — zero upfront cost)
- **Typical payback:** 3-5 years for residential, 2-4 years for commercial
- **No permits required** for rooftop solar under 200 kW in Thailand

### Best Posting Times (Thailand, GMT+7)
- **Facebook:** Tue-Thu, 11:00-13:00 and 19:00-21:00
- **Instagram:** Mon-Wed, 10:00-12:00 and 18:00-20:00
- **Best engagement days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Avoid:** Late night (after 22:00), early morning (before 08:00), Sunday mornings

## Content Strategy

### Brand Voice
- **Professional but approachable** — like a knowledgeable friend, not a corporate brochure
- **Educational first** — teach, don't sell. Build trust through expertise
- **Local** — Koh Phangan specific. Island life, tropical climate, eco community
- **Bilingual** — English primary caption, Thai translation below (separated by line break)
- **Clean aesthetic** — no emoji overload. Maximum 2-3 per post. Premium feel
- **Data-driven** — use real numbers: kWh, THB savings, kWp systems, payback years

### Content Pillars (rotation)
1. **Educational** (30%) — How solar works, savings calculator, net billing explained, inverter comparison, panel technology
2. **Local Impact** (20%) — Koh Phangan energy costs, island sustainability, eco-tourism connection, property value
3. **Behind the Scenes** (15%) — Installations, equipment arrival, team at work, site surveys
4. **Customer Stories** (15%) — Testimonials, before/after energy bills, project spotlights
5. **Industry News** (10%) — Thai energy policy, PEA updates, technology trends, EV infrastructure
6. **Tips & Tricks** (10%) — Maintenance, maximizing self-consumption, reading your electricity bill

### Posting Frequency
- **20 posts over 30 days** (~5 posts/week)
- Mix of formats: single image, carousel, Reels, Stories
- Primary: Facebook (broader Thai reach) + Instagram (visual portfolio, younger/expat audience)

- **Layout:** Title slide → content slides → CTA slide

- **Slide count:** 4-5 slides

- **Size:** 1080x1080px

- **Style:** Split-screen or centered focal stat
- **Key numbers in orange or green, contrasting with navy background

- **Size:** 1080x1080px
- **Layout:** Large quote text, source attribution, subtle brand elements

- **Layout:** Numbered tips with icons, one or two tips per slide
