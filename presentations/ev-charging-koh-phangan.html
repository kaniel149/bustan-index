Risk Analysis

סיכונים ומענה

HIGH
Grid Power Reliability — Island grid can be unstable
→ Solar + battery buffer provides 80% energy independence. UPS for critical systems.
HIGH
Low Initial Demand — Few EVs on island currently
→ Fleet rental creates guaranteed demand. Phase deployment with demand growth.
MED
Regulatory Changes — EV incentive policies may shift
→ Front-load imports while subsidies active. BOI promotion application for tax benefits.
MED
Competition Entry — National chains (EA, PTT) may expand
→ First-mover advantage + location lock-in + solar cost edge. Brand loyalty program.
MED
Tropical Climate Wear — Heat, humidity, salt air
→ Marine-grade enclosures. IP65+ rated equipment. Quarterly maintenance schedule.
LOW
Currency Fluctuation — THB/USD/CNY exposure
→ Revenue in THB, costs mostly in THB. CNY exposure only at import time.
LOW
Technology Obsolescence — Charger standards evolving
→ CCS2/GB/T dual-standard chargers. Modular design allows upgrades.
