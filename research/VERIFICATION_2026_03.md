10. **Module prices significantly lower than stated:** Document uses THB 7-9M/MW for modules. Current system costs should be THB 4,000-8,000/kWh ($115-230/kWh) depending on configuration and sourcing**
12. **Total CAPEX per MW likely lower:** With module price corrections, total CAPEX should be THB 14-20M/MW rather than THB 17.8-25.2M/MW for ground-mount solar
